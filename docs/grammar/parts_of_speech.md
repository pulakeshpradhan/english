# The COMPONENT Codes (Parts of Speech)

!!! info "The Class Definitions"
    **Objective:** Identify the function and class of every variable (word) in the sentence string.

## 1. The Core Variables

### Nouns (The Objects)

* **Function:** Names a person, place, thing, or idea.
* **Types:** Common (*city*), Proper (*London*), Abstract (*freedom*).
* **Code:** "The **server** is down."

### Pronouns (The Pointers)

* **Function:** Replaces a noun to avoid redundancy.
* **Types:** Personal (*he, it*), Possessive (*mine*), Reflexive (*myself*).
* **Code:** "**It** is not working."

### Verbs (The Functions)

* **Function:** Expresses action or state of being.
* **Types:** Action (*run, compile*), Stative (*know, exist*), Auxiliary (*be, do, have*).
* **Code:** "She **wrote** the script."

## 2. The Modifiers

### Adjectives (The Attributes)

* **Function:** Modifies (describes) a Noun or Pronoun.
* **Code:** "The **fast** processor."

### Adverbs (The Parameters)

* **Function:** Modifies a Verb, Adjective, or another Adverb. (How? When? Where? To what degree?).
* **Code:** "It runs **quickly**." / "It is **very** fast."

## 3. The Connectors & Locators

### Prepositions (The Coordinates)

* **Function:** Shows relationship (Time/Space) between a noun/pronoun and another word.
* **Code:** "The file is **in** the folder." / "See you **at** noon."

### Conjunctions (The Logic Gates)

* **Function:** Joins words, phrases, or clauses.
* **Types:** Coordinating (*FANBOYS - and, but, or*), Subordinating (*because, if, although*).
* **Code:** "Fast **but** unstable." / "It failed **because** of a bug."

### Interjections (The Interrupts)

* **Function:** Expresses strong emotion. Independent of syntax.
* **Code:** "**Wow**, that worked!" / "**Oops**, error."
