# Verbs in English

Verbs describe actions or states of being. This section covers the core concepts you need to know about them.

## Key Types of Verbs

1. **Action Verbs**: Physical or mental actions (run, think, decide).
2. **Linking Verbs**: Connect a subject to more information (is, seem, become).
3. **Helping Verbs**: Assist the main verb (have, be, do).

## Common Verbs Explained

- **Go, Get, Take**: These verbs have many uses in English. Check out the specific lessons for each.
- **Regular vs. Irregular**: Learn the past tense forms (walk/walked vs. go/went).
