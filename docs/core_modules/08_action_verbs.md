# Part 8: Action Verbs

!!! info "The Execution Protocols"
    **Objective:** Master the high-frequency utility functions that drive 80% of operations.

## 1. The GET Protocol (The Fetcher)

* **Logic:** To Obtain, Become, or Arrive.
* **Variations:**
  * **Obtain:** "I need to **get** a job."
  * **Become:** "I am **getting** tired." (State change).
  * **Arrive:** "When did you **get** home?"
  * **Passive Voice:** "He **got** fired."

## 2. The TAKE Protocol (The Input)

* **Logic:** To perform an action, consume, or transport.
* **Variations:**
  * **Time:** "It **takes** 10 minutes."
  * **Action:** "**Take** a break." / "**Take** a look."
  * **Transport:** "**Take** the bus."

## 3. The GO Protocol (The Movement)

* **Logic:** Movement away from current position.
* **Structure:** Go + Preposition.
  * **Go To:** Destination (*Go to work*).
  * **Go For:** Purpose (*Go for a run*).
  * **Go On:** State (*Go on holiday*).
  * **Go + Ing:** Activity (*Go swimming*).
