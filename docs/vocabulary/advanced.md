# Advanced Vocabulary

Level up your English with sophisticated vocabulary.

## C1/C2 Level Words and Phrases

Replacing common words with more precise ones can dramatically improve your writing and speaking.

| Common Word | Advanced Word | Example |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Clear** | **Evident** | It was evident that he was lying. |
| **Dangerous** | **Hazardous** | The chemicals are extremely hazardous. |
| **Important** | **Essential** | Good nutrition is essential for growth. |
| **Rich** | **Affluent** | She comes from an affluent family. |
| **Use** | **Utilize** | We must utilize our resources wisely. |
| **Show** | **Demonstrate** | Let me demonstrate how this works. |

## Power Words for Business

- **Collaborate**: Work together.
- **Implement**: Put into effect.
- **Strategize**: Create a strategy.
- **Analyze**: Examine in detail.

Expand your vocabulary daily!
