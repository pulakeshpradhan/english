# Parts of Speech

Words are categorized based on their function in a sentence. These categories are called Parts of Speech.

## Major Parts of Speech

1. **Noun**: A person, place, thing, or idea. (e.g., *cat*, *London*, *happiness*)
2. **Pronoun**: Replaces a noun. (e.g., *he*, *she*, *it*, *they*)
3. **Verb**: An action or state of being. (e.g., *run*, *is*, *jump*)
4. **Adjective**: Describes a noun or pronoun. (e.g., *blue*, *tall*, *happy*)
5. **Adverb**: Describes a verb, adjective, or another adverb. (e.g., *quickly*, *very*, *well*)
6. **Preposition**: Links a noun to another word. (e.g., *in*, *on*, *at*, *for*)
7. **Conjunction**: Joins words, phrases, or clauses. (e.g., *and*, *but*, *because*)
8. **Interjection**: Expresses emotion. (e.g., *Wow!*, *Ouch!*)

Understanding these building blocks is essential for sentence construction.
