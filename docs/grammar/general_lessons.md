# General Grammar Lessons

Miscellaneous grammar rules, tips, and explanations aggregated from the [Grammar Lessons Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLzVm1SmjPKc_OnC56Bb5G2jhqDA_UNAOZ).

This section acts as a miscellaneous bin for all those little grammar questions that don't fit perfectly into a larger category like 'Tenses' or 'Parts of Speech'.

- **Prepositions of Time/Place**: In vs. On vs. At.
- **Active vs. Passive Voice**: Focus on the action or the actor.
- **Direct vs. Indirect Speech**: Reporting what someone said.

Explore the playlists for more topics.
