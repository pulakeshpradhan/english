# Common English Sentences

Phrases and sentences used frequently in daily conversation that you can learn by heart.

From the [Common English Sentences Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLzVm1SmjPKc_OnC56Bb5G2jhqDA_UNAOZ).

## Daily Communication

### Greetings

- **How's it going today?**
- **What have you been up to?**
- **Long time no see.**

### Expressing Opinion

- **To put it simply, ...**
- **As far as I'm concerned, ...**

### Ending a Conversation

- **It was nice chatting with you.**
- **I'd better get going.**
- **Catch you later.**

Practicing entire sentences helps your brain recognize patterns faster than learning isolated words.
