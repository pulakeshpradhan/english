# General Vocabulary Lessons

Diverse vocabulary lessons from [Vocabulary Lessons Playlist](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLzVm1SmjPKc_OnC56Bb5G2jhqDA_UNAOZ).

## Topics

* **Synonyms**: Improve your descriptions with better words (big -> enormous, good -> excellent).
* **Antonyms**: Opposites (hot/cold, fast/slow).
* **Categories**:
  * **Feelings**: Happy, Sad, Anxious, Excited
  * **Weather**: Sunny, Rainy, Humid, Freezing
  * **Travel**: Baggage, Check-in, Departure
  * **Business**: Meeting, Agenda, Strategy

Consistent practice is key to expanding your word bank.
